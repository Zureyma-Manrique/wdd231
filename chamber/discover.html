Discover Timbuktu

Sankore University

Address: Sankore District, Timbuktu

Description: One of the three ancient centers of learning in Timbuktu, an intellectual and spiritual center.

Djinguereber Mosque

Address: Yubu-Kaina District, Timbuktu

Description: A famous learning center of Timbuktu, built in 1327, made from mud-brick and organic materials.

Sidi Yahya Mosque

Address: Saray-Kaina District, Timbuktu

Description: Dating back to 1440, this mosque is a key part of Timbuktu's historic scholarly community.

Grand Marché

Address: Central Square, Timbuktu

Description: The bustling central market where you can find local crafts, foods, and textiles.

Manuscript Libraries

Address: Various Locations

Description: Home to thousands of priceless ancient manuscripts on science, medicine, and philosophy.

Tuareg Culture Experience

Address: Sahara Desert Outskirts

Description: Visit a traditional encampment to learn about the history and culture of the Tuareg people.

Houses of Explorers

Address: Old City District, Timbuktu

Description: See the historic homes where famous explorers like Heinrich Barth and René Caillié stayed.

Niger River Port

Address: Korioumé, near Timbuktu

Description: The historical gateway to Timbuktu for salt caravans and trade along the Niger River.
